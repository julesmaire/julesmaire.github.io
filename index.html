Jules Maire

Postdoctoral researcher in cryptography at ENS Ulm.
Research interests: multiparty computation, zero-knowledge proofs, privacy-preserving technologies.

My work focuses on the design of efficient and provably secure cryptographic primitives enabling privacy-preserving computations, with emphasis on secure multi-party computation and zero-knowledge protocols.
